Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

TripSync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data: We use your browser's "localStorage" to save your trip projects and search history. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except for the specific search queries you send to our AI engine.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., clicks on affiliate links) to understand how our service is used and to improve our offerings.

2. How We Use Information

We use your search queries to generate AI-powered travel recommendations. We use click data to track the performance of our affiliate partnerships.

3. Data Storage

Your travel plans are stored locally in your browser. If you clear your browser cache or "local storage," this data will be permanently deleted unless you have exported it.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to third-party travel providers (e.g., Booking.com, Agoda, Expedia). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at wcommu@gmail.com.
